Common Questions

FAQ

How much does a tattoo cost?

Small pieces start at $100 minimum. Custom work is priced by the hour ($150-$200/hr depending on artist). Large pieces quoted during consultation.

Do you do walk-ins?

Yes. Walk-ins welcome for flash designs and smaller pieces. For custom work or specific artists, book in advance — some artists are booked 4-8 weeks out.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Eat a full meal beforehand. Moisturize the area for 1-2 weeks prior. Avoid alcohol 24 hours before. Wear comfortable clothes that allow easy access to the tattoo site.

How long does healing take?

Surface healing takes 2-3 weeks. Full deep healing takes 3-4 months. We provide detailed aftercare instructions after every session.

Do you tattoo darker skin tones?

Absolutely. All our artists are experienced tattooing all skin tones and will select the right ink pigments and techniques for your complexion.

Can you cover or fix an old tattoo?

Yes. Cover-ups and touch-ups are available. Send a photo through our booking contact and we will advise before you come in.

What is your age policy?

You must be 18+ with valid government-issued ID. No exceptions.

Is the studio clean and safe?

Yes. Licensed by NYC Department of Health. Single-use needles only. Hospital-grade autoclave sterilization. Full NYC health code compliance.
